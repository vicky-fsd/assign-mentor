URLs

Deployed URL : https://assign-mentors-2es8.onrender.com

1. To create Student : https://assign-mentors-2es8.onrender.com/student

2. To create Mentor : https://assign-mentors-2es8.onrender.com/mentor

3. To Assign a student to Mentor : https://assign-mentors-2es8.onrender.com/mentor/:mentorId/assign

4. To Assign or Change Mentor for particular Student : https://assign-mentors-2es8.onrender.com/student/:studentId/assignMentor/:mentorId

5. To show all students for a particular mentor : https://assign-mentors-2es8.onrender.com/mentor/:mentorId/students

6. To show the previously assigned mentor for a particular student : https://assign-mentors-2es8.onrender.com/student/:studentId/pMentor
